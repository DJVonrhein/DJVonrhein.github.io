Privacy Policy

The Clef Converter collects no personal data of any kind. Users have no data stored or sent via APIs. There is no third party sharing as it is an offline app not affiliated with any other parties. We do not encrypt data stored because the only data comes from user submitted content from the Files app or Photos app. Any change in this privacy policy will be shared through a warning in app.
